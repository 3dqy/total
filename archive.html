IEEE Winter Program

The IEEE_KSC has held their yearly winter training program, taking place from 21/01/2024 till 25/01/2024. The program was divided into 5 days visiting different facilities. A number of active students have participated in the program with high spirit. Through the trip, we have gained knowledge about topics related to the generation , distribution, and control of kuwait’s electricity. In addition to that, we have been introduced to the production process of switchgear units and power transformers The program provided a rich environment of socializing, knowledge and experience.
